Dice Roller

Ever gone to a friends house to play Dungeons and Dragons and you are sitting there with your laptop about to create your character when you realize That you forgot your dice?

Well good news you can use my dice roller program. Just click on the appropriate number of sides for example if you want to roll a 4 sided die click on Roll d4, and the program will a 4 sided dice for you.
